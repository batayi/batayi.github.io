About

I am a Geospatial and UAV Engineer with experience in UAV flight operations, 3D point-cloud processing, and remote sensing. After a BSc in Geomatic Engineering in Ghana, I moved to Germany for an MSc in Geodetic Engineering (University of Bonn) with a focus on Mobile Sensing and Robotics and remote sensing. My current work centres on multi-temporal point clouds, topographic change detection, and environmental monitoring. I am interested in roles in UAV flight testing, 3D mapping, and remote sensing R&D in Europe.

UAV Projects

Selected work focused on UAV flight testing, BVLOS/VLOS operations, and drone-based mapping and data acquisition.

Flight Testing & QA

VTOL Hybrid & Fixed-Wing Flight Testing (Ghana)

Planned and executed VLOS and BVLOS test campaigns for VTOL hybrid and fixed-wing UAVs as Team Lead at SKT AeroShutter. Designed test plans, performed pre-flight and post-flight checks, analysed PX4 flight logs, and reported issues to improve system reliability and safety.

UAV Mapping

Corridor & Site Mapping for Infrastructure Clients

Led UAV photogrammetry missions for infrastructure and mining clients, from mission planning and GCP collection to data processing. Produced orthomosaics and DSM/DTMs in Pix4D/Metashape and integrated results into GIS for engineering design and monitoring.

Operations

Operational Procedures & Checklists for UAV Teams

Developed standardised pre-flight, in-flight, and post-flight procedures for multi-person UAV teams, including risk assessment, equipment checks, and log-based issue tracking, improving mission consistency and reducing operational incidents.

Geospatial & Remote Sensing Projects

Projects focused on 3D point clouds, change detection, deforestation, and global agro-environmental analysis.

3D Point Clouds

Topographic Change Classification from Multi-Temporal Point Clouds

MSc thesis project on the Isar River (Germany), using multi-temporal UAV photogrammetry and TLS data. Applied DeepCluster 3D Change Detection with a Siamese KPConv backbone to classify erosion, deposition, vegetation change, and stable surfaces from high-resolution point clouds.

Remote Sensing

Deforestation Prediction with Sentinel-1 & Environmental Variables

Built a deep learning model combining Sentinel-1 radar imagery, environmental covariates, and satellite alerts to predict near-term deforestation risks in tropical forests in Laos and Colombia.

Global Data

Global GMO Adoption & Forest Cover Integration

Processed multi-year global forest cover rasters and GMO adoption maps to build a panel dataset at national and subnational level. Used R, Python, and GIS to aggregate, normalise, and visualise long-term forest trends and agricultural intensification.

Web GIS

Web GIS & Spatial Database Applications

Designed and implemented Web GIS applications using PostgreSQL/PostGIS, GeoServer, and Leaflet. Focus on serving raster and vector layers through OGC services and building interactive dashboards for exploration of environmental and socio-economic data.
