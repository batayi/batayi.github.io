About

I am a Geospatial Engineer with experience in 3D point-cloud processing, change detection, and remote sensing. After a BSc in Geomatic Engineering in Ghana, I moved to Germany for an MSc in Geodetic Engineering (University of Bonn) with a focus on Mobile Sensing, Robotics, and remote sensing.

My current work centres on multi-temporal point clouds, topographic change detection, and global agro-environmental analysis. I am interested in roles in 3D mapping, geospatial data science, and remote sensing R&D in Europe.

Geospatial & Remote Sensing Projects

Selected work focused on 3D point clouds, change detection, deforestation, and global agro-environmental analysis.

3D Point Clouds

Topographic Change Classification from Multi-Temporal Point Clouds

MSc thesis on the Isar River (Germany) using multi-temporal UAV photogrammetry and TLS data. Developed an unsupervised 3D pipeline (Siamese KPConv + DeepCluster) to classify erosion, deposition, vegetation change, and stable surfaces from high-resolution point clouds.

3D Modelling

3D Model of BOST Fuel Storage – Akosombo, Ghana

Led a small team to capture high-overlap UAV imagery over BOST’s fuel storage depot and jetty in Akosombo. Planned and flew missions with a DJI enterprise drone, then processed the data into a dense point cloud, textured 3D mesh and orthomosaic. Delivered accurate 3D site data to support inspection, planning and safety analysis.

UAV Mapping

Health Facilities Mapping for Zipline – Ghana

Led a team to map health centres across multiple regions in Ghana to support Zipline’s emergency medical delivery operations. Planned and flew UAV surveys around each facility and processed the imagery into high-resolution orthomosaics, giving planners clear spatial context to design safe flight paths for medical drops.

Web GIS

Web GIS for Experimental Fields – Campus Klein Altendorf

Built a Web GIS prototype that links a WFS field layer with relational database tables (fields, species, treatments). Implemented a Java backend to generate GeoJSON, drive popups, ontology links and search, so researchers can explore field trials through an interactive map.
