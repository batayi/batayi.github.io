UAV Mapping
Role: UAV Engineer / Survey Lead – SKT AeroShutter
Deliverables: Orthomosaics, DSM/DTM, contours, volume reports

Overview

I led UAV-based photogrammetry projects for infrastructure and mining clients, including corridor mapping (roads, pipelines, powerlines) and detailed site surveys. The goal was to provide accurate geospatial data for design, monitoring, and construction planning.

Workflow

  • Mission planning for coverage, GSD, overlap, and flight safety.
  • Ground control point (GCP) planning, surveying, and QA.
  • Image acquisition with multirotor and fixed-wing platforms.
  • Photogrammetric processing in Pix4D/Metashape: alignment, dense cloud, DSM/DTM, orthomosaics.
  • Exporting products and integrating them into QGIS/ArcGIS for client use.

Technical Stack

  • UAVs: multirotor and fixed-wing mapping platforms.
  • Processing: Pix4D, Agisoft Metashape, CloudCompare.
  • GIS: QGIS, ArcGIS, PostGIS for storing and serving results.

Impact

The projects reduced traditional survey time, improved spatial coverage, and provided high-resolution data that could be revisited for monitoring. Clients used the outputs for corridor design, cut/fill analysis, and progress tracking.
