Operations
Role: UAV Team Lead – SKT AeroShutter
Focus: Safety, repeatability, incident reduction

Overview

Beyond flying, I worked on the “process layer” of UAV operations: defining checklists, standard procedures, and documentation to make multi-person UAV missions safer and more repeatable across projects and pilots.

Key Contributions

  • Pre-flight, in-flight, and post-flight checklists (airframe, payload, GNSS, batteries, telemetry).
  • Risk assessment templates considering airspace, obstacles, weather, and ground risk.
  • Standardised logging for incidents, near-misses, and maintenance events.
  • Briefing and debriefing routines for field teams.

Impact

These procedures helped reduce operational incidents, improved communication between pilots and observers, and made it easier to on-board new team members into complex UAV projects.
