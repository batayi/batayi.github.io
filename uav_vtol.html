Flight Testing & QA
Role: UAV Engineer / Team Lead – SKT AeroShutter
Location: Ghana · Platforms: VTOL hybrid & fixed-wing · Stack: PX4, Pixhawk, Mission Planner, QGroundControl

Overview

As UAV Engineer and Team Lead at SKT AeroShutter, I planned and executed test campaigns for VTOL hybrid and fixed-wing UAVs used in mapping and inspection projects. The focus was on validating new platforms, firmware updates, and mission profiles under real-world conditions before deploying to client projects.

My Responsibilities

  • Designing and documenting test plans for performance, endurance, and reliability checks.
  • Conducting VLOS and BVLOS test flights with ground crew coordination and risk assessment.
  • Performing systematic pre-flight, in-flight, and post-flight checks based on written checklists.
  • Downloading and analysing PX4/ULog flight logs to identify anomalies and regressions.
  • Reporting issues and suggested fixes to hardware and software teams and OEMs.

Technical Stack

  • Flight control: PX4, Pixhawk-based autopilots.
  • Ground control: Mission Planner, QGroundControl.
  • Data: ULog/PX4 logs, telemetry, GNSS/IMU status, battery metrics.
  • Tools: Excel and Python scripts for log post-processing where needed.

Impact

The structured test campaigns helped catch firmware and integration issues early, reduced in-field failures, and improved standard operating procedures for the UAV team. This experience is directly transferable to systematic flight testing and QA roles in R&D teams.
