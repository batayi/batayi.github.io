UAV Mapping
Client: Zipline · Location: Multiple regions across Ghana
Role: Project lead for UAV data acquisition and orthomosaic creation

Overview

  • Goal – Map health facilities across multiple regions in Ghana to aid Zipline's emergency medical delivery operations.
  • Data – UAV imagery collected around each health facility to provide clear spatial context for flight planning.
  • Workflow – UAV flight planning → data capture → orthomosaic generation → integration into Zipline's planning system for delivery routing.
  • Result – A high-resolution orthomosaic, enabling Zipline planners to design precise and safe flight paths for medical deliveries.

Data Acquisition

  • Coordinated with Zipline to identify key health facilities across regions in Ghana.
  • Planned UAV flight paths to ensure full coverage of facilities and surrounding areas for accurate delivery planning.
  • Captured high-quality imagery using a DJI Matrice drone, ensuring high overlap for photogrammetric processing.

Processing & Mapping

  • Processed images into a high-resolution orthomosaic using photogrammetry software (e.g., Agisoft Metashape).
  • Performed post-processing to ensure that all key facilities were accurately represented and georeferenced.
  • Provided the final output as a seamless, georeferenced orthomosaic ready for integration into Zipline's system.

Impact & Results

  • Delivered a precise, up-to-date orthomosaic to Zipline, enabling more efficient and accurate emergency medical deliveries.
  • Enabled Zipline to optimise flight path planning and ensure the safety and efficiency of their UAV delivery network.
